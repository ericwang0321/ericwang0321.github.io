结论先行：AI 公司的 GTM 不是"要不要销售"，而是让获客与交付的摩擦匹配客单价。个人工具靠产品自传播；API 平台用开发者试用筛选生产需求；法律、政府与训练数据则依赖领域专家、可信度和高触达交付。 The takeaway up front: GTM for AI companies isn't a question of whether to hire salespeople — it's about matching acquisition and delivery friction to deal size. Personal tools spread themselves; API platforms use developer trials to surface production demand; legal, government, and training-data businesses run on domain expertise, credibility, and high-touch delivery.

CRO 不是"销售老大"，而是收入系统的总设计师A CRO Isn't the "Head of Sales" — They're the Architect of the Revenue System

Chief Revenue Officer 的核心责任：把市场、销售、渠道、定价、客户成功与收入运营（RevOps）整合成一个可预测、可扩张的系统。VP Sales 管"这个季度签多少单"；CRO 管从需求产生到续约扩张的整条收入链——包括决定哪些客户根本不该由销售碰（自助）、哪些交给伙伴、定价怎么配合销售动作。

A Chief Revenue Officer's job is to fuse marketing, sales, channels, pricing, customer success, and revenue operations into one predictable, scalable system. A VP of Sales owns "how many deals close this quarter"; a CRO owns the entire revenue chain from demand creation to renewal and expansion — including deciding which customers sales should never touch (self-serve), which go to partners, and how pricing supports the motion.

每周真正做的事What They Actually Do Every Week

  • 选择 ICP 与分层：哪些客户自助，哪些交给销售，哪些需要伙伴。
  • 决定组织容量：AE、SE、CS、伙伴经理在哪些地区和行业配置。
  • 管理漏斗与预测：pipeline coverage、转化率、销售周期、赢单/输单原因。
  • 把定价、包装、采购门槛和产品路线连起来。
  • 把成交后的采用率、续约、扩张和 NRR 当作同一条收入链管理。
  • Pick the ICP and segment it: which customers self-serve, which get a rep, which need a partner.
  • Set org capacity: where to place AEs, SEs, CS, and partner managers by region and industry.
  • Run pipeline and forecast: coverage, conversion rates, cycle length, win/loss reasons.
  • Wire pricing, packaging, procurement thresholds, and the product roadmap together.
  • Treat post-sale adoption, renewals, expansion, and NRR as the same revenue chain — not someone else's problem.

Genspark：一个正在发生的案例Genspark: A Case Study in Progress

Genspark 是典型的"PLG 长出企业需求后才请 CRO"。其首任 CRO（Jamison Powell，2026.6 上任）的公开动作包括：搭建 RevOps、GTM Engineering、Scaled Revenue、CX/CS 领导层；补 B2B Marketing 与 Partnerships；建立客户分层、经销结构和全球覆盖；把 PLG 注册到企业扩张统一为一条漏斗。

Genspark is the classic pattern: PLG generates enterprise demand first, then the company hires a CRO. Its first CRO (Jamison Powell, June 2026) has publicly focused on standing up RevOps, GTM engineering, scaled revenue, and CX/CS leadership; adding B2B marketing and partnerships; building account segmentation, reseller structures, and global coverage — unifying everything from PLG signup to enterprise expansion into a single funnel.

这说明 CRO 的价值不是"亲自多签几单"，而是把已经出现的产品需求转成可重复、可预测的企业收入。 The point: a CRO's value isn't closing a few more deals personally — it's converting product demand that already exists into repeatable, forecastable enterprise revenue.

"一个工程师 + 一个律师"为什么可能够用Why "One Engineer + One Lawyer" Can Actually Be Enough

公开资料不支持把它理解为"整个客户交付只有两个人"。更准确的理解是：面向单个客户的小型 pod（一名 Legal Engineer + 产品/工程支持），背后是共享的产品、模型、安全、集成和客户成功平台。人少不是省钱，而是产品标准化 + 角色合并 + 客户自己出人三件事叠加的结果。

The public record doesn't support reading this as "two people run the entire account." The accurate picture: a small per-customer pod (one Legal Engineer plus product/engineering support) sitting on top of a shared product, model, security, integration, and customer-success platform. The small headcount isn't cost-cutting — it's what you get when product standardization, role consolidation, and customer-supplied staffing stack on top of each other.

人数少的四个根本原因The Four Reasons the Pod Stays Small

  • 卖的是标准化 SaaS 平台——主要工作是配置与推动采用，不是为每家律所重写一套系统。
  • 律师身份合并了多个角色：discovery、可信演示、提示/工作流设计、质量判断，减少交接损耗。对律所来说，"对面坐着一个懂行的前律师"本身就是信任来源。
  • 模板、Playbook、Office/知识库集成和最佳实践跨客户复用——第 50 家律所的交付成本远低于第 5 家。
  • 客户自己的创新、IT 和业务 champion 承担大量内部推广。Harvey 的高价筛选出了愿意配人配资源的客户。
  • It's a standardized SaaS platform — the work is configuration and driving adoption, not rebuilding a system for every firm.
  • The lawyer collapses several roles into one: discovery, credible demos, prompt/workflow design, and quality judgment, with no handoff loss. And for a law firm, having a knowledgeable ex-lawyer across the table is the trust.
  • Templates, playbooks, Office/DMS integrations, and best practices are reused across customers — the 50th firm costs far less to deliver than the 5th.
  • The customer's own innovation, IT, and practice champions do much of the internal rollout. Harvey's premium pricing filters for customers willing to staff it.

什么时候两个人不够When Two People Stop Being Enough

  • 大量定制集成、数据迁移、复杂权限或跨国数据驻留。
  • 多个业务线同时上线，且客户内部缺少 champion。
  • 产品准确率仍需大量人工补偿，或每个用例都要重新开发。
  • 目标从"工具采用"升级成"重构法律服务交付模式"。
  • Heavy custom integrations, data migration, complex permissions, or cross-border data residency.
  • Multiple practice groups launching at once with no internal champion.
  • Model accuracy still needs heavy human compensation, or every use case requires fresh development.
  • The goal shifts from "adopt a tool" to "restructure how legal services are delivered."

此时 pod 会扩编，或引入合作伙伴——这也是 Anthropic 建 Claude Partner Network 的同一逻辑：企业交付需求超过厂商自身产能时，伙伴生态就是产能模型。 At that point the pod grows, or partners come in — the same logic behind Anthropic's Claude Partner Network: when enterprise delivery demand outstrips the vendor's own capacity, the partner ecosystem becomes the capacity model.
